Upcoming Event

New Hope Children Home

Kawangware -Nairobi

30th March, 2024

Join us on a heartwarming journey to New Hope Children's Home, where love knows no bounds and smiles light up the room! Nestled amidst the serene countryside, New Hope Children's Home is not just a shelter; it's a sanctuary where every child's dream is nurtured with compassion and care.

At Die Hard Children Foundation, we believe in the transformative power of love and support. Here, children find solace, security, and a sense of belonging. From providing a safe haven to offering quality education and healthcare, every aspect of their well-being is meticulously attended to.

During our visit, we'll have the opportunity to interact with the resilient young souls who call New Hope their home. From playful laughter to inspiring stories of courage, each encounter promises to leave an indelible mark on our hearts.

Together, let's extend a helping hand and bring a ray of hope into the lives of these beautiful children. Whether through sharing a meal, engaging in fun activities, or simply lending a listening ear, our presence can make a world of difference.

Join us as we embark on this meaningful journey to New Hope Children's Home, where every visit is not just an act of kindness but a celebration of love, hope, and the boundless potential of every child."
